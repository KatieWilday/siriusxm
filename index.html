New Music!

Shawn Mendes - I'll See You Soon

Shawn Mendes is returning with new music! The “Lost in Japan” singer-songwriter is priming the Mendes Army for another album. He revealed this and more on Friday (February 21) in an Instagram Stories video, confirming his fourth studio album is coming down the pipeline. “What’s up guys? I know that I haven’t been on Instagram at all lately,” he begins in the clip. “But I just wanted to say that I love, and I miss you so much. I’m making another album. So, yeah, I love you and I’ll see you soon!” While the 21-year-old crooner held out on exactly when longtime listeners can expect his new material to arrive, it goes without saying that Mendes’ followers are going to be on pins and needles until then.
